Blue Magic
By Peoharen
Read Me | Updates | Sub Systems | Installation
 
    Blue Magic
      If you have never played Final Fantasy then I'm sure you have no clue what Blue Magic is but you have seen it's system before. Blue Magic is the ability to permanently learn a spell or effect a monster uses. While the system to learn changes, FFVI it was attack the monster, FFXIII it was use an item, FFIV it was eat the monster, and so on. FFV in which it debuted and several other FF games it is based on getting hit with the attack and surviving. I have done this for UO.

      Blue Magic's spells are not just about damage. Many are utility, status, or just plain operate outside the normal principles spells use. Be sure to check the gimmicks attached to each.

      Blue Magic in its default set up works kinda like a class system many shards use. If you cast a Blue Spell and you have over 50.0 (excluding items) in another spellcasting or taming skill. The idea for this lock is based off synergy limitation. It simply takes less skills and stats to be a spellcaster allowing more combinations than a combat specialist. Rather than out right blocking the combinations I went with limiting their ability to hit GM in those skills. Also it forces the intent that you should pick up 30.0 in magery and use that system for recalling instead of creating another recall rip off.

      Many words are italicized, mouse over them if you want an explanation.
    General Features

      Minimal edits to RunUO's files
      The only absolutely required edit is done to Scripts\Misc\AOS assuming you've patched up to handle SA support. This edit allows programmers to patch into the various enhancement properties AOS, SE, ML, and SA have added without having to equip a made up item into the Unsed_F slots, which may at any point be put into use by OSI anyway.

      Seperate Save File
      Installing, updating, removing, is pretty easy. Blue Magic creates and maintains it's own separate save file that tracks who is a blue mage and what spells do they know. The only stuff saved in game is the additional items, mobiles, and some spawners (which can be removed with a single command). No edits have to be made to BaseSpell, Regents, or anything of the like.

      30 New Spells (and growing)
      Cleric & Druid sport less than 10 and each has been around for 4 years.

      Several New Monsters
      Some of the monsters have their own cool traits.

      New Quests
      Various quests have been added for blue mages. Not a blue mage? No problem, a few are slayer type and open for others to participate in for rewards.

      Status System
      The status system is no longer required for use but to me it's effects are superior than to the alternative use of enhancement properties. should you take the time to install it, make the system true and several spell effects will adapt and use it.

    Spells

      Angel's Snack
      Consumes a Heal, Cure, and Refresh Potions heal several aliments and a minor amount of HP. Applies to your entire party!

      Auto Life
      If used on living targets they gain an auto-life buff that can ressurect them upon death.

      Bad Breath
      The caster exhales a cone of gas that can debuff speed, combat skills, and resistance to the affected mobiles

      Blow Up
      The caster charges up and then detonates him self dealing twice his HP in damage to the surrounding area.

      Demi
      Halves the HP total of a target (max 150hp loss), no damage rights given

      Dragon Force
      A stackable self buff that bestows melee poweress at the cost of spellcasting (you may use special moves). On the 7th casting and beyond it's effect changes to shooting fireballs.

      Drain Touch (move)
      A melee attack that bestows negative levels and heals the user.

      Fifty Needles
      Shoots several needles at a target, each one dealing one damage per hit.

      Flamethrower
      Breath a wide line of fire burning everything in it's path.

      Frog Drop
      Drops frogs in an area. Duration (thus damage) & range are based on several uncommon items.
      Items: World Map, Red Leaves, Sand, Special Hair Dye, Rope, Vines, Tribal Paint, a Rock, doesn't have a Runebook, doesn't have gold.

      Goblin Punch (move)
      Adds a random multiplier to your damage.

      Guard Off
      Halves the resistance of a target.

      Level 4 Holy
      If the target's skill total can evenly be divided by 4 it deals a massive amount of damage.

      Limit Glove (move)
      Deals the percentage difference in your current and maximum HP.

      Magic Hammer
      Halves the mana of the target (max loss is 50).

      Matra Magic
      Swaps the mana and HP of the target, anything that can have more than 500HP is immune.

      Mightyguard (status)
      (Status) Bestows Protect & Shell on the target.
      (Other) Increases your resistance, a GMed Magic Resist character should hit 60% Resist All with this spell.

      Mind Blast
      Damages the target's intelligence, the more spellcasting skills they have the greater the effect.

      Night
      Darkens the area and puts everyone nearby (including the caster and his allies) to sleep.

      Poison Claw (move)
      Infects the target with poison.

      Shadow Flare
      Deals a large amount of damage to an area.

      Shield (status)
      (Status only) While under it's effects damage taken drains your mana instead. the spell ends when you run out of mana.

      Stare
      Confuses a single target.

      Switch
      Swap places with another creature.

      Thurst Kick (move)
      A powerful attack that slides your opponent away from you.

      Trine
      Layers several explosive runes on the ground which explode after some time. The runes can also explode by walking over them making them a quick trap.

      Vanish
      Hides the caster and shunts them over a few spaces in a random direction, the caster may then sneak away for a few steps.

      White Wind
      Heals all friendly creatures in range, amount is based off the caster's current HP.

    Quests

      Blue Ben
      Is the quest offerer for all blue mage quests.

      Blue Mage Training Quest
      Available only if your Forensics skill is below 51.0 (allowing a 50.0 start out)
      It is a skill training quest, reward is the same as EvaInt's (ring of the savant)

      An Empty Vessel
      Only offered if you have more than 51.0 in Forensics.
      Not exactly a quest. It is a series of questions asked by Ben.
      Upon completion of the questions you are offered the Blue Mage Serpent Quest.

      Blue Mage Serpent Quest
      Your answers to An Empty Vessel determine which place you must search for your mark.
      Your mark is a land tile (or there of) that blends in with all other land tiles.
      Only Staff and the player can see it, others walk through and cannot making it the first ever instance item in UO.

      Blue Touched
        Blue Clothing is a special kind of clothing, it comes with Resist All +5 and is blessed. The garment has been Touched by the Azurian forces to be adaptable and enchantable. There are nine different enhancements, Insight, Haste, Arcane, Skill, Mighty, Nimble, Intelligent, Regeneration, and Resistance. Each quest below rewards the items and enhancements.

        Blue Mage Tier One Slayer Quest
        Kill a few drakes, gazers, lichs, and daemons for a random blue clothing reward.

        Blue Mage Tier Two Slayer Quest
        Kill a few dragons, elder gazers, lich lords, and ice fiends for a random enchantment deed.

        Blue Mage Tier Three Slayer Quest
        Kill a few shadow wyrms, beholders, ancient lichs, and balrons for a random powerful enchantment deed.
        Limits Surpassed
        8 items & 9 enhancements right? Not so much. The 9th item, the necklace, can only be enhanced by a special enhancement that cannot be applied to the rest of the clothing.
      Finding Quina
      You require this quest to enter the area where Quina spawns and complete it upon killing him. There is no kick out timer or anything so you may farm him but leaving will prevent reentry for a few days.

      Last Move
      If you have learned all the moves of blue magic you can try a boss fight. Invite your friends and fight for a chance for an artifact. Last Spell

    Items

      Blue Mage Clothing
      The magic binding this clothing into creation is very delicate. Someone untrained in it's use (ie not a blue mage) will unravel it destroying the clothing and all of it's enhancements.
      Forks!
      Like Quina, many blue mages have found a war fork perfectly suited to their combat style. these forks use Wrestling and do not prohibit use of combat maneuvers.
      Silver: Undead slayer.
      Bistro: Imposes a -15% fire resistance penalty on hit.
      Gastro: The animation changes on each swing, each type has it's own bonus.
      Needle: A special fork, exceptional against certain monsters whom may be required by the blue touched quests.
