Blue Magic
By Peoharen
Become A Blue Mage | Using [BM | Learning Spells | Quests | For Others | Development
 
    The Blue Magic Expansion expands on the usage of the NPC Guilds in game.
    NPC Guilds operate independently of the Player Guild system allowing a player to join both. In order to join an NPC Guild you find the desired guild master and say his or her name along with the word join. It costs a tiny 500gp to join an NPC Guild and other than the exception of the Thieves guild there are no prerequisites. In order you change your NPC guild you'll have to find a guildmaster for the guild you're in and state his or her name along with the word resign or quit. It's that easy.

    So if it's that easy why use it? Well, once you join an NPC Guild you cannot change you're guild for a week. This presents an either/or choice in which benefit you want to use and the ability to semi-freely swap between them. In a way it limits you, but it doesn't impose the penalty of wrong choice though out your character's lifespan of use.

    Here is a list all off the current benefits offered though the Blue Magic Expansion by guild.

    Healers
      * Required for the Divine Avenger addon.
        * Each ability uses a number of Holy Points, they are regained slowly.
          * Lay On Hands: A special self healing ability.
          * Smite Evil: Gives a bonus to attack & damage against Red Named targets.
          * Divine Sacrifice: Lose HP to deal more damage.
          * Divine Might: Bestows a Strength boost that ignores cap.
          * Turn Undead: An area Paralyze that only works on undead.
          * Special Mount: Calls a special mount for a duration.
        * You lose access to this if you are a red.
        * Access to these abilities is done though a menu with macroable options.
      * Quitting the Guild doesn't remove the mount, however it will continue to time out and vanish.

    Mage
      * The Mage Guild offers a few small bonuses.
        * Necromancy: You can use a special animation spell to create a small army of lesser undead.
          * As an ultimate sacrifice, an necromancer can trade part of his life to make them more powerful.
        * Magery: No Bonuses assinged for this skill set at this time.
        * Spellweaving: You can use a Focus Gem to create an Arcane Focus without other spellweavers.
          * The gem can be used any anyone with access in your house.
          * The gem can be used once per six hours.

    Rangers
      * Required for the Ranger addon.
        * You can make any weapon Legendary.
          * Legendary weapons are a copy of the original weapon used (uniquely coded effects cannot be copied).
          * While using your Legendary Weapon you gain a bonus to attack and damage based on your Tracking skill.
        * You can pick one region to excell in once per 3 days.
          * In this region you gain a bonus to Pet related skills based on your Camping.
          * Upon entering your region you gain a bonus to your Hiding, this bonus is loss when you leave.

    Thieves
      * Required for the Assassin addon.
        * Allows you to take quests based on espionage such as.
          * Without being seen, pick the door to an NPC's house and obtain the item there in.
          * Stealing from an NPC whom you are not allowed to kill.
          * Sabotage a device or mechanism.
          * Assassinate a target in one hit.
        * Enbles use of the Assassin Command
          * Allows you to use several combat moves no matter which weapon you are using.
          * Can invoke the Healing powers of the belt, and knife throwing of the vest for macros.
          * You can use the special Assassinate move which provides a high damage bonus against full HP targets.
        * Obtain and use combat oriented gear.
        * Obtain special house decorations.

    Tinkers
      * Required for the Aritificer addon.
        * You can use Spell Binding to craft items.
        * You can craft and use a Rune Bow.
      * Quitting the guild prevents use to the Rune Bow but you may continue to use Golems.

    Warriors
      * Required for the Monk addon.
        * Allows you use the Monk Fists and it's comboes and finsher strike system.
        * Allows you to use the Monk's Belt and Jidz-Tetka.
