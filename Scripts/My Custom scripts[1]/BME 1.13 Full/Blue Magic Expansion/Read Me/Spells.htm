Blue Magic
By Peoharen
Become A Blue Mage | Using [BM | Learning Spells | Quests | For Others | Development
 
    Currently there are 30 "spells" offered, with two more on the way, but not all spells can be learned in game yet. I've only 19 of them in so far and I'm working on more.

    Most spells can be learned by being hit with them (like Final Fantasy V/VII/Tactics) in which case upon being hit by a blue spell cast by an untamed creature (players cannot teach each other spells) you'll get a mess in blue on the lower left side of your screen telling you that you learned a new spell.

    Some spells are never cast at the player, they are curative or a type of buff in nature and a smart creature would never cast such a spell on his enemy. Those spells have to be learned though the [BM Study command. After killing the monster you can invoke the command to study their corpse. In doing so the corpse is marked as if someone used Spirit Speak on it and you'll have a chance at learning the monster's spell. Mindful you cannot study a corpse that has been studied or Channeled (spirit speak) more than once.

    And finally, there is a 3rd way of learning of spells. They can only be learned though quests or special rewards. For example, Auto Life (the ressurection spell) can only be learned by escorting the trapped pixie, found in level 3 of the cave addition, outside of the cave.
