    Monk
      A half-complex Monk system that is all based off an item. This things are pretty complex. It has stances via context menus, level, combos, and takes up multiple layers.

      Multi Layer
        They are marked as the glove layer but they equip a second item to the two handed layer preventing you from using other weapons or drinking potions while using them.
      Level
        The gloves can be set to five different levels, each affects the bonus amounts from stances and the base strike's damage bonus. Level gain is meant to be earn through questing and current I have not made any level up quests.
      Improved Unarmed Strike
        At level 1 they deal 1~4 damage, as per Fists. Each level ups the damage by 3 so at the highest level they have a base min/max range of 16~19.
      Stances
        The gloves can be clicked up and set to different elemental themes. Each also has a capstone trait. The gloves must be at the 5th level to gain that property. Finally each element type has a theme. Air boosts defense and speed while being based off the energy type. Earth handles regeneration. Fire is attack & damage. Water is spell focused and based off cold. Most bonuses granted are 5 + (level x 2) but some are less.
      Combos
        The system tracks the last three strikes you made, if the strikes line up into a valid combo you can use a finisher to excute various effects. Upon being double by the wearer they receive a gump that can look like this.

        A. Strike list: air, earth, fire, water, light, dark.
        B. Currently selected strike is colored red.
        C. Combo chain, colored by element.
        D. Finisher (only appears for valid combos).
        E. Wholeness of Body (turns grey when unable to use).
        The four elemental strikes deal an extra 1d6 damage per level.
        Light is half that amount and is direct, the Dark type deals chaos.
      Finishers
        The Gathering Storm
        Struck target has -10% hit chance for 30 seconds.
        The Trembling Earth
        Struck target has -2 to cast speed & recovery for 30 seconds.
        Breath of the Fire Dragon
        The monk breaths a cone of fire damging everyone in it.
        The Raging Sea
        Struck target is Slowed for 30 seconds.
        Healing Ki
        The monk and his nearby party members heal 1d6 HP per level.
        Dance of Clouds
        The monk and his nearby party members gain +25% defense for 15 seconds.
        Grasp the Earth Dragon
        The monk and his nearby party members gain +5 to all resists for 60 seconds.
        Walk of the Sun
        The monk and his nearby party members gain +10 to all stats for 60 seconds.
        Aligning the Heavens
        The monk and his nearby party memebers gain +25% lower mana cost for 15 seconds.
        Touch of Despair
        Doesn't work yet.
        Falling Star Strike
        Struck target has -25% to defense for 10 seconds.
        Pain Touch
        Struck target has -10 to all stats for 60 seconds.
        Karmic Strike
        Scores a critical hit (*2 damage) but the monk takes a huge penalty to resistance for 5 seconds.
        Freezing The Life Blood
        Struck target is paralyzed for 15 seconds.
      Wholeness Of Body
        Paralyzes you and only works while paralyzed.
        You have increased HP recovery, +20hp/second.
        Duration is based on level and once used it cannot be used again for two minutes.
